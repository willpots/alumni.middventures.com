Since its inception in 2007, the Middlebury College Programs on Creativity and Innovation (PCI) has sought to supplement the traditional Liberal Arts curriculum with series of programs for students to exercise their own creativity, innovation and entrepreneurial inclinations.

Meet the Alumni

Sydney Alfonso
Middlebury '12

Sydney founded Etkie in 2012. Etkie is a jewelry and accessories company that creates handmade, unique pieces in collaboration with female artisans in developing areas. Sydney recently gave a TEDx talk in Hamburg Germany about her experience with entrepreneurship, and Etkie recently became funded by KIVA, a website that connects people to borrowers and partner institutions. Etkie will launch its crowd funding campaign in February on plumalley.co, which is exclusively formed for female entrepreneurs.
